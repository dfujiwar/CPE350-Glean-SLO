Thanks for thinking of GleanSLO and sharing your food with those in need! Please note that GleanSLO is a small volunteer-powered organization with limited staff-time and harvesting resources- therefore unable to guarntee that we will be able to schedule a harvest with you, especially during busy seasons where we mush prioritize properties with larger quantities of produce. See below for other options if GleanSLO is unable to schedule a harvest with you. Also note that for those harvests we are unable to schedule, our equipment is limited and volunteers have limited abilities, so we connot guarantee that we will be able to harvest all the fruit you have to offer, but we will try our best! Thank you for being part of the gleaning movement and sharing with our community. Have you donated to GleanSLO in the past but want to let us know your crop is ready to pick this season? You may already be registered! Please contact Program Coordinator Emily Wilson, ewilson@slofoodbank.org or (805)235-1180, to arrange a glean.

All harvest participants will be trained in proper harvesting methods and sign waivers of liability that protect the crop owner as well as GleanSLO.Privacy: Information entered here is used soley by GleanSLO and will not be available to the public. We do not share, sell or otherwise distribute personal information.

If you have questions, please contact us at gleanslo@slofoodbank.org

Currently, as a program of the SLO Food Bank, GleanSLO harvests according to storage and distribution capacity of the food bank and its partner agencies. We do not currently harvest loquats, wine grapes, olives, or any type of nuts due to either very short shelf life, need for processing, lack of demand, or potential allergy issues.
